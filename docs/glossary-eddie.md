Dictionary Entry
----------------

The main content of a [dictionary]. Consists of the [head], the
[orthothograpy] representation, the [part of speech], and the
[definitions].

### Part of

 * **1** [dictionary]

### Contains

 * **1** [head]
 * **1** or more [definitions]
 * **1** [part of speech]
